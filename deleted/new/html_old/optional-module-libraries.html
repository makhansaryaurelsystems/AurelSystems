Add-on Module Libraries

The following (optional) libraries of process modules are available as add-ons to the standard CADSIM Plus process simulation package:

Power Library
  • Burner/Combustor
  • Compressor
  • De-Super Heat Calculation
  • Ejector
  • Evaporator
  • Gas Header
  • Turbine
  • Recover Furnace
  • Steam Accumulator
Fiber Library

Washers:

  • Drum Washers *
  • Decker *
  • Atmospheric or Pressure Diffusers *
  • Wash Presses *
  • Wash Zone *

* Washers can be customized for specific manufacturer and individual performance parameters. CADSIM Plus standard modules, such as High Density Tank and Towers, complete the fiber line simulation requirements.

Bleach Plant:

  • Oxygen Delignification
  • D Stage bleaching
  • E stage bleaching
  • EOP stage bleaching

Fiber:

  • Sorb/Desorb module for ionic exchange
  • Pressure Screens
  • Shrinkage and yield loss
  • Cleaners
  • Chip Refiner
  • Pulper
  • Pulp Dryer
  • Pulp / Reject Refiner
  • Stone Grinders
  • Saveall/Thickener
